Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is GERMBox?

GERMBox is a platform for exploring and investing in Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN)-backed financial instruments like Treasury Bills, Open Market Operations (OMO), and Bonds. It's designed to make fixed-income investments accessible and transparent.

Is GERMBox a real investment platform?

No, this is a client-side demo showcasing mock data and features. In a real scenario, investments would be processed through secure, regulated channels.

How do I start investing?

On the homepage, browse available instruments, filter by type, sort by rate, and click "Invest" to open the Quick Invest modal. Enter your amount and currency, then submit (mock process).

What currencies are supported?

We support NGN, USD, and EUR. Exchange rates are fetched in real-time and displayed in the ticker.

Why isn't the map showing institutions?

Ensure your browser allows location access and that the Mapbox token is valid. The map displays mock CBN institution locations across Nigeria.

How is my portfolio managed?

Investments are saved locally in your browser (mock). View them on the Portfolio page, where a chart shows value over time.

What if I encounter an error?

Check your internet connection for API-dependent features like exchange rates. For other issues, clear browser cache or contact support.
